Company Overview

Shri Swami Samarth Engineers Limited was incorporated on May 10, 2019, following the conversion of the partnership firm M$. Shri Swami Samarth Engineers under the Companies Act, 2013. Established in 1998, we are a trusted Class I-A Government Contractor registered with the Public Works Departments of Maharashtra, Karnataka, and the Central Government.

We undertake major infrastructure projects, including national highways, flyovers, and bridges, on an EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) basis. We also deliver complete civil engineering solutions for government irrigation works such as dams, canals, tunnels, KT, rivers, and PDN structures. Backed by years of experience and a highly capable team, we ensure precise planning, quality workmanship, and technical excellence in every project we execute.
