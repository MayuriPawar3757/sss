      About

      Shri Swami Samarth Engineers

      Shri Swami Samarth Engineers Limited was incorporated on May 10, 2019 by conversion of a partnership firm “M/s. Shri Swami Samarth Engineers into the Company as “Shri Swami Samarth Engineers Limited” under the provisions of Companies Act, 2013.

      • Specializing in National Highways, bridges, and EPC-based infrastructure projects.
      • Executing government projects like dams, barrages, irrigation tunnels, K.T. weirs, and canals.
      • Delivering quality infrastructure for the Govt. of Maharashtra.

      Since the incorporation of our firm in 1998 we are registered as Class I-A Government Contractors with Public Works Department, Govt. of Maharashtra, Govt. of Karnataka and Central Government.

      Services

      Shri Swami Samarth Engineers

      Sewerage

      We provide end-to-end solutions for industrial and municipal sewerage treatment projects. Our expertise includes sewage systems, effluent treatment, and desalination plants. With a strong value-chain approach, we handle everything from design to commissioning, ensuring efficient delivery and sustainability in all types of treatment infrastructure works. We also prioritize energy-efficient systems that reduce costs and environmental impact.

      Water Irrigation

      Water is one of the earth’s most vital resources. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation as an experienced and reliable agency in executing water irrigation solutions. We have successfully delivered a wide range of related projects. Our ability to consistently meet high standards has made us a trusted name in the water infrastructure sector. We focus on maximizing agricultural output and rural livelihood through effective irrigation networks.

      Bridge Construction

      Shri Swami Samarth Engineers' heritage in structural design and support services for execution distinguishes us as a leader in the construction of elevated corridors such as flyovers, bridges, etc. The vertical has strong expertise in the design and construction of a wide array of civil structures like flyovers, viaducts, bridges, etc. Our emphasis on safety, longevity, and innovation ensures every structure stands the test of time.

      Canals Construction

      We have built a reputation of being reliable & trustworthy canal development contractors. We are now in the process of moving our services into neighboring states. As a measure of maintaining our standards as a leading & reliable contractor, we have an in-house architectural team and offer a service of complete design & build for all types of works. We ensure water reaches even the most remote areas through efficient and durable canal systems.

      Barrages Construction

      Shri Swami Samarth Engineers is witnessing strong momentum in barrage construction, making a difference with projects that deliver lasting value and innovation. Barrages and dams help store water, supporting thermal power plants and benefiting nearby villages. We enable efficient water use and build for a sustainable future. Our integrated approach supports regional development and long-term water security.

      Bot

      Build Operate Transfer (BOT) is a delivery model where a private partner is given rights to finance, design, build, and operate infrastructure for a fixed time. We have executed BOT projects by aligning with government specifications, ensuring operational efficiency. Our approach balances public interest with long-term, sustainable infrastructure growth. Through BOT, we take accountability for delivering quality infrastructure that serves generations.

      Road Construction

      The construction and maintenance of roads have been and shall remain a capital-intensive continual process that is at the core of any development activity. To enhance the quality we have continuously strived and kept ourselves updated with the technology in terms of material, machinery, and process. We hold a very strong position in BOT projects. Each road we build is a step forward in connecting people, markets, and opportunities.
